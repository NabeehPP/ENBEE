Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our workflow, ongoing hosting costs, and ownership.

What do I need to provide to start? +

You only need to share basic info about your business: logo, services, pictures of your business, and preferred colors. If you don't have text written, we can write it for you!

Are there any hidden monthly charges? +

No. We charge a one-time flat fee. The only ongoing cost is the domain name (approx. ₹800/year) and cloud hosting (approx. ₹1,200/year), which we set up directly in your name so you own it 100%.

Will my website work well on mobile phones? +

Absolutely. Over 85% of local searches are on mobile. Every site we build is fully optimized to look beautiful and load rapidly on smartphones and tablets.

Can I update the website content myself later? +

Yes. We build in a way that allows you to easily edit text, modify menu items, and update images without touching any code. We will provide a 10-minute video walkthrough guiding you how.
